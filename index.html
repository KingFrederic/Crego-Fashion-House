The Curriculum

Nine Modules.
Complete Mastery.

```
01
Module 1
The Foundation — Block Essentials
  • Precision body measurement for any body type
  • The Master Bodice Block (front & back)
  • Skirt & Trouser Block drafting
  • Sleeve mastery & bicep adjustments
02
Module 2
Pattern Manipulation — The Designer's Secret
  • Dart magic — shoulder, side & waist positions
  • Slash & spread for gathers, pleats & flares
  • Princess seams, yokes & asymmetrical necklines
03
Module 3
Trend-Focused Drafting — Viral Styles
  • Structured corsets & bustiers
  • 360/720 peplums, bishop & puff sleeves
  • Wrap dresses & A-line silhouettes
04
Module 4
Advanced Concepts & Workflow
  • Copying ready-to-wear into patterns
  • French curves, notches & tracing wheels
  • Fit correction before cutting fabric
05
Module 5
Basic Blocks & Slopers
  • Step-by-step bodice block drafting
  • Foundational skirt patterns for all body types
  • Basic sleeve pattern drafting
  • Accurate body measurement techniques
06
Module 6
Pattern Manipulation Techniques
  • Advanced dart manipulation methods
  • Adding fullness — slash & spread techniques
  • Style line development from basic blocks
07
Module 7
Specific Garment Drafting
  • Jean-cupped corsets & strapless tops
  • 360, 720 & 1080 peplum construction
  • Patterned wrapped A-line dresses
  • Complex custom designs from client sketches
08
Module 8
Beginner Essentials
  • Introductory pattern making guides
  • Tools — rulers, pattern paper & French curves
  • Fixing common drafting mistakes
09
Module 9
Advanced & Creative Techniques
  • 3D pattern cutting & TR cutting
  • Draping vs. flat pattern making
  • Deconstructing garments into base patterns
```
